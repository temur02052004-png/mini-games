How to play

  • Two players share the screen — White moves first.
  • Click a piece to see its legal moves, then click a highlighted square.
  • All standard rules are enforced: check, castling, en passant, and promotion.
  • You can't make a move that leaves your own king in check.
  • The game ends on checkmate or stalemate.
