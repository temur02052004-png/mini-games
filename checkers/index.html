Rules

  • Two players share the screen — Red moves first.
  • Men move diagonally forward one square; kings move both ways.
  • Jump an adjacent enemy piece into an empty square to capture it.
  • Captures are mandatory, and you must keep jumping with the same piece if you can.
  • Reach the far row to become a king (crowned piece).
  • Win by capturing all enemy pieces or leaving them with no move.
